## What commercial projects do you handle?

We handle all commercial electrical projects including ground-up construction, panel upgrades, HVAC systems, emergency power installations, lighting retrofits, and tenant improvements for offices, retail spaces, restaurants, and industrial facilities.

## Do you work with general contractors?

Yes, we regularly collaborate with general contractors and construction managers. We coordinate seamlessly with other trades and provide detailed electrical plans, load calculations, and code-compliant installations from foundation to inspection.

## How long does a commercial project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. Small panel upgrades may take 1-2 days, while ground-up construction electrical systems can take several weeks to months. We provide detailed schedules and work efficiently to minimize business disruption.

## Are your electricians licensed and bonded?

Absolutely. All our highly skilled commercial electricians hold proper state licenses, are fully insured, and bonded. We maintain all required certifications and stay current with commercial electrical codes and safety regulations.

## Can you handle emergency repairs?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency service for commercial clients. Whether it's a power outage, equipment failure, or safety hazard, our experienced team responds quickly to minimize downtime and get your business back to normal operations.

## Do you provide maintenance plans?

Yes, we offer comprehensive preventive maintenance programs tailored to commercial facilities. Regular inspections and maintenance help prevent costly breakdowns, ensure code compliance, and extend the life of your electrical systems.
